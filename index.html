I am a quantum scientist. I work at the interface between physics, computer science, mathematics, and sometimes also philosophy of science. I am currently a Hartree Postdoctoral Fellow @ the Joint Center for Quantum Information and Computer Science of the University of Maryland & NIST. I did my Ph.D. @ Freie Universität Berlin in Jens Eisert's group, and studied physics, philosophy, and mathematics @ Konstanz, Oxford and Munich.

Please feel free to email me at dhang@umd.edu.
